Kisii Sports Club, is in many ways the ultimate embodiment of the Western Kenya’s unique marriage of ancient golf tradition and a modern 9-Hole Championship Golf Course.

Founded in 1914, our members-only club is a sanctuary for golf enthusiasts and families alike. Nestled in the heart of Kisii, we offer a premier golfing experience along with world-class amenities for all ages. Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or just starting out, Kisii Golf Club is your perfect escape to leisure, fitness, and community. Join us and become part of our storied tradition and vibrant community today.

Attractions & Activities

Located close to your all your favourite game drive spots in Western Kenya

Kisii Golf Club is your perfect base for exploring the stunning beauty and attractions of Western Kenya. Just a short drive away, you can experience the world-renowned Masai Mara National Reserve, famous for its incredible wildlife and the annual Great Migration. Beyond the Masai Mara, discover the vibrant culture of Kisii with its unique soapstone carvings, and explore nearby treasures like the Nyangweta Forest and the scenic Nyakwana Falls.

Western Kenya offers a plethora of attractions easily accessible from our club. Visit Ruma National Park to see the rare Roan antelopes, or enjoy bird-watching and fishing at Lake Victoria. The bustling port town of Kisumu, with its lively markets and the Kisumu Impala Sanctuary, is also within reach. After your adventures, return to the comfort and luxury of Kisii Golf Club, where you can relax in our top-notch amenities. Make your stay extraordinary by combining world-class golf with unforgettable safari experiences.
