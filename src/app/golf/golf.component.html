Kisii Sports Club, is in many ways the ultimate embodiment of the Western Kenya’s unique marriage of ancient golf tradition and a modern 9-Hole Championship Golf Course.

Founded in 1914, our members-only club is a sanctuary for golf enthusiasts and families alike. Nestled in the heart of Kisii, we offer a premier golfing experience along with world-class amenities for all ages. Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or just starting out, Kisii Golf Club is your perfect escape to leisure, fitness, and community. Join us and become part of our storied tradition and vibrant community today.
